Adding Passive Sensors (version 5.1.0 or higher)

After installing or upgrading a Passive Sensor (version 5.1.0 or higher), the sensor needs to be enrolled to a specific Command Center:
  1. Login to the Command Center
  2. Navigate to the page Sensors > Sensors overview
  3. In the Secondary Navbar, click New sensor > Enrollment Overview
  4. Inside the table, find the respective sensor (it's Enrollment Status should be "Pending")
  5. Select the sensor by pressing the radio button on its left
  6. Click on the Actions button in the upper-right corner of the table
  7. From the drop-down, pick Accept request
  8. Review the information in the popover modal window and click Accept request
This will ensure that the appropriate Sensors is enrolled in the Command Center.
