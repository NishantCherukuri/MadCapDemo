Home

The Home page provides a comprehensive overview of your network with graphical widgets that provide an overview of Assets, Alerts, Vulnerabilities, Sensors and Command Center Health, and other system observations.

The Home page displays the following:

Metrics:

  • New assets
  • New alerts
  • Non-compliant assets
  • Sensor health
  • Command Center warnings

Graphs:

  • Assets at risk
  • Alerts over time
  • Top vulnerabilities
  • Top system observations

It is possible to filter the above information by the last 24 hours, 7 days or 30 days by using the filter drop-down in the top left corner of the Home page.
